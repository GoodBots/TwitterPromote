@PromoteInSthlm, etc

A twitter bot made by the Bots saving the World organisation. Promotes local events and corporations that, one bit at a time, also Save the world.

Automatically retweets any tweets that uses the hashtag #STHLM. Blocks users with non-philanthropic commercial interests. Please follow or support by thanking us.

Bots saving the World is a small community for creating bots with a good purpose. If you share this vision be in touch and maybe join us in hacking.
